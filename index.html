Our Story

From Our Kitchen to Your Table

A Chicago institution for 70 years — where every guest is a neighbor.

Meet Angelo

At Yellow Rose Cafe, we believe the best meals are served with a side of conversation and a lot of heart.

What started as a dream to create a true neighborhood gathering spot has grown into a cornerstone of our Elston Avenue community. Our owner and chef, Angelo, has always operated with one simple philosophy: treat every guest like a neighbor, and every meal like a home-cooked Sunday feast.

"Whether you're catching up with old friends, starting your workday, or stopping by for one of our signature daily specials, Angelo and our team are here to make sure you feel right at home."
